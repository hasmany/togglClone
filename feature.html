One-click time tracking

With Toggl you track time in real time. You never lose a minute of your billable time. If you forget to switch it on, then enter time later on. Organize your time by projects or tags, and mark it as “billable.”

Ideal for teams. Boost group productivity.

Toggl is built for teams. Your entire staff will be set up and running in minutes. It’s as easy as Twitter - no training required. Start out simple, and drill deeper with user rights and project setup later on.

SEXY REPORTS.TRANSPARENT BILLING.

Get an instant overview of your billable time and team progress. Export timesheets. E-mail (or print) professional reports directly to customers or bosses. There are dozens of cool Pro features you’ll grow to love.

TOGGL ANYWHERE

Your time entries are synced in real time. Offline support keeps you toggling even when out of Wifi or mobile coverage areas. Hit the button on your iPhone and the timer automatically starts on your computer. That simple. Oh, and Toggl plays well with your Trello, Asana or other favorite productivity tool.
